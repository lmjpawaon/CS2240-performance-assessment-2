The Ateneo Circle of Computer Studies Students is the sole academic club of the Computer Studies Cluster of the Ateneo de Davao University . As an academic club, our main goal is to provide our members with opportunities and initiatives for academic and extracurricular growth; in order to get ahead in the academe and to excel in the workplace.
